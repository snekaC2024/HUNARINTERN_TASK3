Frequently Asked Questions

What is Netflix?

Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more.

How much does Netflix cost?

Watch Netflix on your smartphone, tablet, Smart TV, laptop, or streaming device, all for one fixed monthly fee. Plans range from ₹149 to ₹799 a month.

Where can I watch?

Watch anywhere, anytime, on an unlimited number of devices. Sign in with your Netflix account to watch instantly on the web at netflix.com from your personal computer or on any internet-connected device.

How do I cancel?

Netflix is flexible. There are no contracts and no cancellation fees. You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks.

What can I watch on Netflix?

Netflix has an extensive library of feature films, documentaries, TV shows, anime, and more. You can explore various genres and find what you love.

Is Netflix good for kids?

Netflix has a dedicated kids' experience with content suitable for kids. You can create separate profiles and set parental controls.

Can I watch on multiple devices?

Yes, Netflix allows you to watch on multiple devices depending on your subscription plan, ranging from one to four simultaneous streams.

Can I download movies and TV shows?

Yes, you can download select TV shows and movies to watch offline, available on the Netflix app for iOS, Android, and Windows 10.
